What Subflow Does

Four things. That's all it takes.

No bloat, no learning curve — just the tools that move renewal rate.

📲

WhatsApp renewal reminders

Auto-send personalised reminders 1, 3, or 7 days before renewal — with your customer's name, product, price, and date. 90%+ read rate vs 8% for email. Customers tap Confirm or Pause right in chat, no login needed.

🌐

Hindi, Hinglish & English templates

Pre-approved WhatsApp templates in all three — ready from day one, no Meta review delays. Your Tier 2 customers in UP, Bihar, Rajasthan get messages in the language they actually respond to.

⏸️

Pause & skip — not just cancel

Customers who want to cancel often just need a break. Give them a self-serve pause or skip in WhatsApp. Saves the subscription without you lifting a finger — and without an awkward support call.

🛡️

Opt-in only. Max 2 msgs per renewal.

Only messages customers who opted in at checkout. Hard cap of 2 messages per renewal cycle. One-tap STOP opt-out always available. Your brand's WhatsApp reputation stays clean — not flagged as spam.

Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 2025 · Operated by AdityaxInnovations, Pune, India.

This policy explains how Subflow collects, uses, and protects your information. Plain English — no legalese where we can help it.

1. Who we are

Subflow is developed and operated by AdityaxInnovations, Pune, Maharashtra, India. Questions? Email privacy@subflow.app.

2. What information we collect

2.1 Shopify store data

When you install Subflow, we access the following from your store with your permission:

  • Store name, URL, and owner contact details
  • Order data — IDs, line items, customer names, phone numbers, and emails — for tracking subscription renewals
  • Product and variant information to identify subscription-eligible products
  • Customer phone numbers, used solely to send WhatsApp renewal reminders

2.2 WhatsApp Business data

If you connect your WhatsApp Business API account, we process message delivery receipts (sent, delivered, read) to show you engagement metrics. We do not store message content after delivery.

2.3 App usage data

We collect anonymised usage events to improve the product. This data is not linked to individual customers of your store.

3. How we use your data

  • To operate Subflow's core subscription tracking features
  • To send WhatsApp renewal reminders on your behalf
  • To generate analytics visible only to you in your dashboard
  • To send product updates, billing notifications, and support communications
  • To comply with legal obligations

We do not sell, rent, or share your data or your customers' data with third parties for marketing purposes.

4. Data storage and security

Data is stored on Render (render.com) and MongoDB Atlas, both maintaining industry-standard security. Data is encrypted in transit (TLS 1.2+) and at rest (AES-256). We retain data for as long as your account is active, plus 30 days after you uninstall.

5. Third-party services

  • Shopify — store data access via the Shopify Admin API
  • Meta (WhatsApp Business API) — message delivery for renewal reminders
  • Render — cloud infrastructure and backend hosting
  • MongoDB Atlas — database storage

6. Your customers' rights

As a Subflow merchant, you are the data controller for your customers' personal information. You are responsible for ensuring your customers have consented to receiving WhatsApp messages from your brand before enabling Subflow's reminder features.

7. Your rights

You have the right to access, correct, or delete data we hold. Email privacy@subflow.app — we respond within 30 days.

8. Changes to this policy

We'll notify you of material changes via email or in-app notification at least 14 days before they take effect.

9. Contact

privacy@subflow.app · AdityaxInnovations, Pune, Maharashtra, India.
